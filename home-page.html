The company 3A is one of the leading vehicle recycling units in Attiki.

With years of experience in the field, highly trained staff and state-of-the-art factory equipment can guarantee the provision of high-quality services and the excellent handling of its operations

We fully accept the receipt and permanent deletion of your car by legally granting destruction / permanent deletion certificates.

Our company is active in the recycling of old vehicles since 2007, applying modern methods of recycling with respect to the environment and human beings. It operates in conjunction with Greece's only licensed and licensed Alternative Vehicle Management System (AVMS), complying with European environmental and safety guidelines.

Our goal is to be the most reliable solution for recycling your car, above all with respect to the environment and human beings.

For the more efficient service of our customers, the company expands throughout Attica. Based in Maroussi, Attica our two branches are housed in Metamorphosi and Kalyvia Thorikou.
