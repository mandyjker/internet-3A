Reward table

With every vehicle you recycle, you get a reward.

The amount of the reward depends on how many years you own the vehicle and how bad its condition is (50% - bad condition, 80% - average and 100% - good condition). For further information, you can see the table below.

Vehicle's age Condition Max reward
0-5 50%-80%-100% 800
5-10 50%-80%-100% 1000
10-20 50%-80%-100% 1200
20+ 50%-80%-100% 1000

Cost of tow-truck

If you are not able to drive your vehicle to the nearest station, then you can make an appointment for a tow truck to pick it up.

The cost of the tow truck will be subtracted from your reward and it depends on the distance from your house to the nearest recycling station.

Distance Cost
less than 10 km 20 euros
10-30 km 30 euros
30-100 km 40 euros
>100 km We don't go that far
