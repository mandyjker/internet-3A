Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I recycle my vehicle?

There are 5 different stations in Attiki, Greece. By signing in, you can see all the addresses. You can sign in here.

What do I earn by recycling my vehicle?

First of all, clear consciousness. Also, some money. You can check the link here for more information.

My car's condition is too bad to drive it to the drop-station. What do I do?

By creating an account, you are able to make an appointment for a tow-truck to pick up your vehicle.

When will I be able to take my delivery certificate?

Three days is the maximum time till the employee submits your data, so in approximately four days you will have it.

What do I do when I take my delivery certificate?

You have to submit it in the tax office in order to delete your car.
